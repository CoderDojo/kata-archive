**The Design Badges**

These exciting new design badge challenges are intended to encourage
CoderDojo Ninjas to explore their creativity and discover their design
talent using the Autodesk 3D design software. The badges will be awarded
digitally via Credly and the limited-edition physical badges will be
sent to individual Dojos. There are five digital design badges to
collect:

To earn and get awarded these badges (Both Digitally and Physically)
Champions and Mentors need to [Register Your Dojo Here\! ](https://docs.google.com/a/hwf.io/forms/d/1Y4EB5Bdv9ldzp1SQvwFKR3i9WNvb7pTZjWEoA6zFRjk/viewform)

Note: There will be both digital and limited-edition physical copies of
these badges. These badges are available only to individuals aged 13
years and
older.

**Getting started is easy\!**

Autodesk is providing you with all of the tools you need to successfully
bring computer-aided design into your Dojo:

1.  **Learn more, get the software and explore the fun design badge
    challenges** :Start today\! Download the Autodesk® Tinkercad badge
    guide and access the software, supporting step-by-step instructions,
    how-to guides and engaging videos at www.autodesk.com/coderdojo.
2.  **Champions & Mentors – Register your Dojo** :Register your address
    with the CoderDojo Foundation to receive your one-off pack of
    physical stickers [HERE](http://ow.ly/ArRGY)
3.  **Join the Autodesk Education Community** : Learn about the full
    range of Autodesk software and learning solutions and download
    Autodesk 3D design software for your personal computer
    atwww.autodesk.com/education.
